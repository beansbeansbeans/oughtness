About

What is this place?

Inspired by the GifGif and Place Pulse projects, we built this site as a tool for measuring the qualities of criticalness and tractability in causes deemed worthy of global attention by GiveWell, a charity research organization.

Users are asked to compare two causes at a time in terms of their criticalness or tractability. Votes are collected and visualized on the data page, where users can see the causes ranked by priority.

Methodology

It's not obvious how to get from pairwise comparisons to rankings. For this task we turned to the analytic hierarchy process, a widely accepted technique for making complex decisions.
