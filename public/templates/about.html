About

What is this place?

Oughtness is a tool for measuring the criticalness and tractability of causes deemed worthy of global attention by GiveWell, a charity research organization. The site is inspired by the GifGif and Place Pulse projects.

Users are asked to compare two causes at a time in terms of their criticalness or tractability. Votes are collected and visualized on the data page, where users can see the causes ranked by priority.

Methodology

In order to derive overall rankings for causes from pairwise comparisons, we made use of the analytic hierarchy process (AHP). The AHP is a widely accepted technique for making complex decisions involving multiple alternatives and criteria. For more details, check out the methodology section of our data page.
